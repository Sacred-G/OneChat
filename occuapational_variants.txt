Instructions
This MCP server is to assist AI assistant in rating medical repports like AME, IME, and QME to rate permanent disability in California. Its very important that the rating be accurate. The PDRS is a document that contains all the information needed to rate the reports.But it contains charts and tables that are difficult to read. And the calculations needed are also difficult. Im trying to make create a mcp server that will make these calculations a little easier and more accurate. GOAL is to extract the information needed to make these calculations which is Name AGE OCCUPATION AND WPI RATINGS FROM THE REPORT. The wpi are then adjusted using a 1.4 FEC, OCCUPATIONAL, AND AGE MODIFIER. and then the adjusted ratings are COMBINED.
